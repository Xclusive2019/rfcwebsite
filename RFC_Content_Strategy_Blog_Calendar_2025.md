## Executive Summary

This comprehensive content strategy addresses the critical content gaps identified at RFC: no blog presence, no downloadable resources, limited social media strategy, and minimal email nurturing. Based on extensive research into South Africa's food safety regulatory landscape, competitor analysis, and industry trends, this document provides 12 months of blog topics, link-building assets, social media plans, lead magnets, and academy course recommendations.

### Key Research Findings:
- **R638 compliance** is the #1 search topic for South African food businesses
- **Spaza shop registration crackdown** (Nov 2024 - ongoing) has created massive demand for compliance content
- **FSSC 22000 Version 6** (effective April 2024) mandates food safety culture plans - a hot topic
- **Draft R3337 labelling regulations** (expected 2026) will bring front-of-pack warning labels
- **COA applications** are a major pain point across all 9 provinces
- **Home-based food businesses** are booming post-COVID with unclear compliance requirements
- Competitor ASC Food Safety dominates SEO through extensive blog content - RFC can differentiate through practical, downloadable resources
