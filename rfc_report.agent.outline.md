# RFC Website Redesign & SEO Strategy Report: Recommendations for Outranking the Competition

## Executive Summary
### Key Findings
#### RFC operates two websites (rfcsa.co.za and rfcacademy.co.za) in a moderately competitive but fragmented South African food safety market
#### The biggest gap identified is absence of blog content, lead capture mechanisms, and modern mobile-responsive design on the main consulting site
#### ASC Food Safety (ascfoodsafety.com) is the dominant competitor with 65+ courses and commanding SEO presence; Entecom competes through 46+ articles and 61+ eBooks
#### RFC Academy has only 3 courses versus ASC's 65+, representing a significant growth opportunity in e-learning
#### This report provides a comprehensive roadmap covering website redesign, SEO fundamentals, content strategy, and lead generation

## 1. Current State Assessment (~2500 words, 2 tables)
### 1.1 rfcsa.co.za Main Consulting Site Audit
#### 1.1.1 Visual design analysis: dark green theme appears dated, hero section lacks clear value proposition and prominent CTA
#### 1.1.2 Navigation structure review: 6 main menu items (Home, About, Services, Food Safety Training, Comply Cloud, Contact) with dropdown submenus
#### 1.1.3 Mobile responsiveness assessment: site lacks mobile optimization, touch targets and text sizing need improvement
#### 1.1.4 Page speed and technical performance: image compression, caching, and CDN implementation needed
#### 1.1.5 Lead generation audit: no visible blog, no lead magnets, limited CTAs, no newsletter signup, no exit-intent offers
#### 1.1.6 On-page SEO gaps: missing schema markup, suboptimal page titles, missing meta descriptions, no FAQ sections
### 1.2 rfcacademy.co.za E-Learning Platform Audit
#### 1.2.1 Modern design with green theme but only 3 courses currently available (Food Defence & Fraud, R638, Food Safety Culture)
#### 1.2.2 All courses priced at R1,600 — mid-market positioning against ASC (R879) and Entecom (R3,393)
#### 1.2.3 SAATCA accreditation displayed but course catalog significantly smaller than all major competitors
#### 1.2.4 Login/Register functionality present but no free content or lead capture for prospective students
#### 1.2.5 Course cards display level, duration, and lesson count but lack reviews, ratings, or social proof
